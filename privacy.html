Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-26

This Privacy Policy explains how ReLife ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the ReLife mobile app (the "App").

Summary

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase verification data may be sent to our server to confirm ownership.
  • If ads are shown, ad partners may collect device identifiers to serve ads.

Information We Collect

On-device data

Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored on your device. This data is used to provide the gameplay experience and is not required to create an account.

Purchase verification

If you purchase DLC or subscriptions, we may send purchase tokens/transaction IDs to our server to verify ownership and restore purchases across devices. The server stores purchase status and entitlement flags linked to a device identifier.

Ads

If the App shows rewarded ads, our ad partners (such as Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers, IP address, and limited usage data to serve and measure ads.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App.
  • To verify purchases and restore entitlements.
  • To show ads and measure ad performance when ads are enabled.
  • To improve the App and fix bugs.

Sharing

We share data with third-party services only as needed to provide purchases or ads. These services include app store billing providers and ad networks.

Data Retention

We keep purchase verification data as long as needed to provide entitlements and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion by contacting us.

Your Choices

  • You can delete the App to remove local game data.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of server-stored purchase data.
