FAQ

I found a bug. What should I send?

  • Device model
  • OS version (iOS/Android)
  • App version
  • Short reproduction steps
  • Screenshot/video if available

Purchases are missing

Open ReLife and use the restore/reload purchases option in Settings. If items are still missing, contact support with platform and purchase details.

Game Center / Google Play Games sign-in issues

Update system services, restart the app, and retry. If the issue persists, send device + OS details to support.
